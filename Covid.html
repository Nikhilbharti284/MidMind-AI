COVID-19 is a contagious disease caused by the coronavirus. Taking preventive measures can significantly reduce the risk of infection and spread.

  • Maintain social distancing (6 feet).

  • Get vaccinated to protect yourself and others.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell or have symptoms.
