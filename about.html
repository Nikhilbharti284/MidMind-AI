Understanding Cancer

Learn about the different types of cancer, their causes, and prevention methods.

What is Cancer?

Cancer refers to a group of diseases involving abnormal cell growth with the potential to invade or spread to other parts of the body. Common types include lung, breast, and skin cancer.

Causes of Cancer

Cancer can be caused by a variety of factors, including lifestyle choices, environmental exposures, and genetics.

Prevention

While not all cancers can be prevented, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, avoiding tobacco, and regular screening can reduce risk.

Breast Cancer Awareness

Early detection saves lives. Learn about breast cancer and how to identify the signs.

What is Breast Cancer?

Breast cancer is a disease in which cells in the breast grow out of control. It is one of the most common cancers affecting women.

Symptoms and Signs

Symptoms may include lumps in the breast, changes in breast shape, or unusual discharge from the nipple.

Early Detection

Regular self-examinations, clinical exams, and mammograms can help detect breast cancer in its early stages.

Mental Health Awareness

Understanding mental health is crucial for maintaining overall well-being.

What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being, influencing how people think, feel, and act.

Common Mental Health Issues

Anxiety, depression, and stress are some of the most common mental health conditions.

Taking Care of Mental Health

Regular physical activity, a balanced diet, good sleep hygiene, and seeking professional help when needed can promote mental well-being.
